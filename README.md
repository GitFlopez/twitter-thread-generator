## Why Twitter Threads?

Twitter/X threads are the highest-ROI content format for building an audience:
- **Threads get 2-6x more engagement** than single tweets
- **Thread readers are high-intent** — they scroll to the end when hooked
- **Threads repurpose into everything** — LinkedIn, email, blog, Reels
- **Building in public threads** drive product signups and follower growth

The problem: most people know threads work but don't know how to write one that gets read past tweet 2.
