## Full Description

Twitter/X threads are the highest-ROI content format for building an audience. They get 2-6x more engagement than single tweets, they compound over time (threads get discovered weeks later), and they repurpose into every other format you need.

The problem: writing a thread that actually gets read past tweet 2 is harder than it looks. Most threads die at tweet 3 because the hook is weak, the structure is off, or each tweet is trying to do too much.

This skill solves all of it.

**4 Thread Formats — Each Purpose-Built:**

**Hook Thread (Authority Building)**
The "I did X, here are N things I learned" format. Establishes expertise, builds followers, drives DMs. 10-15 tweets. Includes hook score analysis and opening tweet optimization.

**How-To Thread (Education + Product Leads)**
Step-by-step instructional format. "Here's how to [achieve result] in [N steps]." Designed to drive signups and build trust before selling. 8-12 tweets. Includes soft product CTA and implementation shortcut.

**Contrarian Thread (Debate + Viral Reach)**
"Hot take: [position]" format. High engagement through disagreement. Includes the nuance caveat that prevents pile-ons, predicted reply patterns, and follow-up thread ideas. 6-10 tweets.

**Story Thread + Repurposing Kit**
Personal narrative format. Hook → struggle → turn → lesson. The most trust-building format on Twitter. Includes the complete repurposing kit: LinkedIn post, email newsletter, blog outline, and Instagram carousel — all from one prompt.

**What Every Thread Includes:**
- Complete thread (all tweets numbered, ≤280 chars each with character counts)
- Hook tweet score and analysis
- Engagement CTA recommendation
- Best time to post
- Hashtag recommendations

**What Makes This Different:**
Most thread generators produce generic content that sounds like every other "here are 10 tips about marketing" thread. This skill is built on actual Twitter/X mechanics: the hook science, the tweet pacing, the CTA psychology, and the reply-bait that gets the algorithm to show your thread to more people.

The repurposing kit (Prompt 4) is the unique differentiator — no competitor at this price generates a full LinkedIn post, email newsletter, blog outline, and Instagram carousel from one thread input.
